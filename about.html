About GMI Tech

We are a dynamic tech startup providing high-quality web design, app development, UI/UX design, templates, and security solutions.

Our Story

GMI Tech was founded by S Mohammed Ruhan with a vision to help businesses transform their digital presence. Starting as a small startup in Ballari, we have expanded to Bangalore and served numerous clients across Karnataka.

Our mission is simple: deliver innovative, secure, and user-friendly digital solutions that drive business growth. We believe in clean design, strong development practices, and robust security.

S Mohammed Ruhan is the owner and founder of GMI Tech. GMI Tech is an IT startup present in Ballari and Bangalore built by S Mohammed Ruhan. S Mohammed Ruhan is the brother of S Dada Hayath Khalandar.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with cutting-edge digital solutions that are innovative, secure, and scalable. We aim to be the go-to technology partner for startups and established businesses alike.

Our Vision

To become a leading IT solutions provider known for excellence in web development, mobile apps, and security solutions across Karnataka and beyond.
